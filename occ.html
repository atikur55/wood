USED CORRUGATED CARDBOARD (OCC)

As a company we strive to not only help local businesses around Gainesville GA and surrounding areas create a zero to landfill solution, but providethe best recyclable material, for local and international recycling facilities.


While most other recycling companies bale all types of mixed corrugated cardboard, we exclusively bale double and triple layered heavy duty industrial corrugated cardboard boxes known as Gaylord, Optibins and Quatro boxes, which are manufactured by International Paper.

Before baling, material is double sorted, for minimal throw-out and moisture readings are taken during every load, we never load anything over 12% moisture.

All our material is kept indoors from the time they arrive at our facility to the time they leave.
